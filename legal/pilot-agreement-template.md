# Pilot Agreement Template (Friendly Tone)

Use this template to capture pilot commitments, confidentiality, and limited liability before inviting Massachusetts customers to test the platform. Replace bracketed placeholders with final values, delete sections that do not apply, and keep a copy for your legal binder.

1. **Overview**

- Company: `TetherTasks` (Massachusetts LLC) invites `[Customer Name]` ("Customer") to try the `TetherTasks` pilot for `[purpose/feedback focus]`.
- Pilot Account Contact: `[Primary Contact Name] <[email]>`

2. **Term & Completion**

- Start Date: `[Pilot Start Date]` End Date: `[Pilot End Date]` (or earlier if either party provides five days’ notice).
- Termination: Either party may end the pilot with five days’ written notice; the Company may also terminate immediately for misuse. After termination Customer agrees to stop using the platform, delete pilot-only materials, and provide any outstanding feedback by `[Feedback Deadline]`.

3. **Pilot Usage**

- License: Company grants a limited, non-transferable license for internal evaluation only.
- Restrictions: Customer will not resell, distribute, embed into production systems, or share access with third parties unless the Company approves it in writing.
- Test Data: Customer will use test/mock data where feasible and only upload data it is authorized to share.

4. **Confidentiality & Feedback**

- Confidentiality: Customer treats the platform, pricing, security posture, and pilot roadmap as confidential. Internal sharing is allowed, but no public disclosure without Company consent.
- Feedback: Customer consents to the Company using feedback and anonymized insights for product improvement, investor updates, or marketing.

5. **Data & Privacy**

- Data Handling: The Company processes pilot data per the [Attach Privacy Addendum or link to Privacy Policy]. Customers must notify the Company of suspected security incidents within `[Incident Notification Hours]` hours.
- Breach Notification: Company will alert Customer of any confirmed breach impacting pilot data within `[72]` hours and assist with mitigation.

6. **Liability & Warranties**

- "As is" Provision: Pilot is experimental; the Company makes no warranties regarding uptime, accuracy, or suitability.
- Limitation: Customer agrees the Company’s liability is limited to the amount Customer actually paid, and excludes indirect, special, consequential, or punitive damages.

7. **Policy Acknowledgements**

- Customer confirms it has reviewed the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy at `[URL to ToS]` and `[URL to Privacy Policy]` and will comply with those acceptable-use rules.

8. **Telemetry & Metrics**

- Monitoring: Company may collect usage telemetry (clickstream, performance, errors) solely to improve the experience.

9. **Optional Reference Clause**

- If a Master Services Agreement (MSA) already exists, this document supplements it and clarifies that the pilot is expressly non-production and limited to evaluation use.

10. **Signatures**

- Company: `[Authorized Signatory Name / Title]`
- Customer: `[Customer Signatory Name / Title]`
- Date: `[Signature Date]`

Include this template in your pilot onboarding packet (PDF or Doc) and ask pilots to sign or acknowledge electronically before provisioning access. Let me know if you want a Notion/Google Doc version or a version with mail-merge fields.
